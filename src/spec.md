# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Add a Contact Us section to the homepage and implement a welcome popup modal to collect visitor contact details.

**Planned changes:**
- Add a Contact Us section on the homepage displaying email address and social media links
- Implement a popup modal that appears automatically on first site visit to collect contact details (name, email, phone number)
- Ensure modal appears only once per session using localStorage
- Style both components following the existing IPL-themed design with cricket blue and energetic orange colors

**User-visible outcome:** Users will see a Contact Us section with contact information on the homepage. When visiting the site for the first time, a welcome popup modal will appear requesting their contact details with a form and close button.
